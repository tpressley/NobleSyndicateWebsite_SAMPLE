Car Club for Hondas and Acuras

This club is about friendships, cars, and a good time. Virginia/Ohio/New York teamed up to have an all HONDA/ACURA exclusive car club that is drama free has built a family like no other.

Requirements: some modifications, active and make it to the few mandatory meets, and make it through the recruiting phase. MUST BE AT LEAST 18‼️

Recruiting phase: this is a 4 week phase that we will see how active you are. We want to keep everyone in the family on their feet and excited about everything coming up!

Banners: are available to purchase with other merch after prospect phase is completed. S, M, and L, available in any color!

If you have any issues please reach out to Founder @Brittanylenay, or Club Prez @Fk8_baymax. All you have to do is download ‘groupme’, signup, let us know your phone number so we can add you to the chats. Welcome to the club!
