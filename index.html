JDM Car Club

This club is about friendships, cars, and a good time. Virginia/Ohio/Tennessee teamed up to have an all JDM car club that is drama free has built a family like no other.

Requirements: leadership approval, make it to a few mandatory meets, and make it through the recruitment phase. We understand that everyone is busy, but we ask that you remain active in the chats, even if its just to say hey here and there. MUST BE AT LEAST 18‼️

Recruiting phase: this is a process we use to see how active you are in chat, see that you are working on making modifications to your car and are a good fit with our club. This phase is depending on how clean your car is and what mods you have when you enter (it can be shortened or extended as leadership sees fit) We want to keep everyone in the family on their feet and excited about everyone builds and up coming meets that each chapter usually make plans to roll with together.

Banners: are available to purchase with other merch after prospect phase is completed. S, M, and L, available in any color!

If you have any issues please reach out to any member of our club leadership: Owner: Brittany- @brittanylenay, Club President: Marcus: @1qwik_fc1 Club VP: Casey: @Club VP: Casey RVA all you need to do is download discord, let me know when you're ready and we'll send you the link to the chats! Once you are in the server, you first will need to change your name accordingly, and then leadership will assign you your role. Once you're in, please read through our "important tings' section.
